Before the course begins, information will be shared via email and the class Google Drive.

  • Trip Guidebook – posted as a Google Drive link and a printed copy handed out at the beginning of class.

Technical references online (you can get hard copies from the NRCS Distribution Center, print, or download to your device so you have access in the field):

3.1 Readings

Assigned readings from a variety of papers and books focused on topics relevant to the location and topic of the day. Copies of the required readings are in the “Syllabus and Course Reader”. The course also carries a book box with many other general references, relevant papers and books, which students can check out. Each day 1-2 papers are required readings that we will discuss over breakfast and/or dinner. The required readings are in the “Syllabus and Course Reader”.

3.2 Course Library

The course also carries a course library box with many other general references, relevant papers and books. Students can check these out for personal reading and as background for their course projects. The contents of the library are listed in the course “Syllabus and readings”.
